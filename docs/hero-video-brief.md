# Royah — Hero Video Brief: "From Paper to Possibility"

A cinematic ~18s loop (or ~35s social promo) showing Oman's journey: today's real Oman →
a spark of transformation → a future Oman that kept its soul. Built to match the website's
Vision-2040 narrative. Real footage (Pexels) + AI shots (Midjourney) + motion (Runway).

## Storyboard (8 shots)

| # | Shot | Source | Runway motion | ~sec |
|---|------|--------|---------------|------|
| 1 | Golden-hour Oman — dunes / corniche / mosque | Real | Slow drone push-in | 2.5 |
| 2 | Hands on a paper ledger, dust, sunlight ("today") | Real | Slow tilt down | 2.5 |
| 3 | Paper dissolves into glowing data/dashboard | AI morph | Paper → screen transition | 2 |
| 4 | Clean app dashboard lights up (echoes Royah Property) | AI | Gentle zoom | 2 |

| 7 | Wide hopeful horizon, sea meets future city at dawn | AI | Slow push, light bloom | 2 |
| 8 | Fade to black → Royah logo + tagline | Render | Hold | 2 |

Shot 1 + the ending black tie together for a seamless hero loop.

## Pipeline
1. Midjourney → still keyframes (16:9).
2. Runway → image-to-video on each still (use the motion column above).
3. Edit/sequence in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve (both free); time to the music beat.
4. Music: Suno / Epidemic Sound / Artlist.
5. Export to spec → drop `hero.mp4` + `hero-poster.jpg` in `public/` → tell Claude to switch it on.
